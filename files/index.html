About the Company

All Phase Contractors, Inc.

Owned & operated by Michael Greig, President

All Phase Contractors was founded on one conviction: homeowners deserve a contractor who actually shows up, stays on the job, and stands behind the work. With more than 20 years of construction experience across the Gulf Coast, Michael personally oversees every project — from the first estimate to the final walkthrough. No hand-offs, no excuses.

Michael personally holds a Certified General Contractor license (CGC#1533136) and a Certified Building Contractor license (CBC#1266054) — the two credentials required to legally build, remodel, and manage every phase of construction under one roof. Structural, finish, and everything in between are handled under one contract and one standard of accountability.
